About Excellence

Excellence started their career at a Daft Punk concert in 2005. The first members were inspired to perform, uncovering without their identities, to prove to their audience that anybody could be a popstar. They started practicing in one of the members’ garage in 2006, trying to find their jam, going from rock music to classic music, becoming acquainted with all of them. By the time they started performing in front of an audience, there was no crowd they could not please, with their concerts transitioning between smooth jazz and hard rock as smoothly as waves on the ocean.

Members

John Singalong Song is the main singer of the band. His voice knows no bounds, and he can even rival Morten Harket when singing Take On Me.

Harold Hayden was previosuly a florist, but is now the drummer of the band, kickstarting the beat under any circumstance.

Reidar is the guitarist. He did not need a better name to be a badass guitarist, he only needed to let it riff.
